Data Cleaning in SQL

In this SQL Server project, the data quality of housing data was improved. The project involved resolving issues related to missing values, duplicates, inconsistent inputs, and column splitting. Missing values were handled using JOIN, ISNULL, and UPDATE statements. Inconsistent inputs were addressed through the utilization of CASE and UPDATE statements, while column splitting was achieved by employing SUBSTRING and PARSENAME. Furthermore, the project utilized CTE to identify and remove 104 duplicate rows.

Data Exploration in SQL

In this SQL Server project, a range of techniques including joins, CTEs, temp tables, windows functions, and aggregate functions were implemented to explore the Covid-19 dataset. Queries were employed to clean, analyze, and visualize the Covid-19 data, focusing on aspects such as death percentages, infection rates, population, and vaccinations at the country, continent, and global levels. These steps were taken to extract the relevant data for visualization on a Tableau dashboard.

Tableau Projects

This tableau dashboard offers a comprehensive view of the Covid-19 pandemic, presenting a wide range of visualizations and data insights. It presents a holistic perspective on global and regional Covid-19 statistics, including total cases, total deaths, and the percentage of the population affected by the virus. Notably, it emphasizes that Europe has the highest death toll, which is eight times greater than Africa's, despite Africa having a population approximately 94% larger than Europe. Moreover, the dashboard features a monthly trend analysis of the infected population and a forecast of the future percentage of the population impacted by the virus.
